About Us

Kerala, is a tropical paradise known for its lush green landscapes, backwaters, and beautiful beaches. Located on the southwestern coast of India, it offers a unique cultural experience with its ancient temples, traditional dance forms, and flavorful cuisine. Experience the beauty of Kerala by taking a leisurely houseboat ride along the backwaters, or immerse yourself in the local art and craft scene.
